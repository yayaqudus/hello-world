Suratul Fatiah

In the name of Allah, The most Gracious, The most Merciful

All thanks is due to Almighty Allah.
The Beneficient, The Merciful.
Master of the day of Judgement.
Ye only shall we worship. And Ye only shall we seek help from.
Guide us to the right path.
The path of those that has been favoured by You.
Not the path of those that has been rejected and cursed.

AMIN
